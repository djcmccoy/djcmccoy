Overview

With general private tutoring, students are exposed to grammar rules, assigned homework, and given guidance on how to further train their skills. This course is designed for all skill levels, ages, and individuals of any native language. The business English course is a separate experience that primarily focuses on students developing confidence, effective, and coherent interview style.

Speaking and writing

Speaking and writing are at the heart of any language. In all course offerings, students will be assessed on their abilities in these areas. Students are taught to use technological resources such as online translators and conjugators. These tools will allow students to methodically review and perfect their future writing.

Document creation

Using Microsoft Office and/or Adobe Creative Suite, students will learn to make modern documents, starting with their own résumé. We will overview design and technical skills will leave students with the skills they need to correctly format and update their résumé, create important business documents such as invoices and memos, and impress potential employers.
